My Mission

After working in multiple development teams of various sizes, in many types of companies I have seen a lot of different leader types. From hands-on lead developer that gets their hands dirty to PMs without any development background.

In more recent years, working as a senior freelance developer I started taking on more project responsibilities and started looking deeper into what I feel like are the best ways to lead a team to successful and sustainable productivity.

As a lead in your company my mission will be to enable my teams to help themselves.That way, instead of spending all day holding the hands of my co-workers on everyday tasks, I will spend my time refining project requirements, optimising productivity and, not at the least, help fostering professional and personal growth for the people and the team.
